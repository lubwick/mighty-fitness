About Mighty Fitness

"Mighty Fitness is more than a training brand — it’s a community built around growth. We’re here for people who want to challenge themselves, stay disciplined, and commit to becoming stronger physically and mentally every day. Through training, accountability, and consistent effort, our mission is to help people build confidence, character, and a mindset focused on long-term growth. "

Who's It For?

  • Athletes wanting to become faster and stronger.
  • Individuals who want to lose weight and have structure and discipline.
  • People ready to get lean and strong.
  • Anyone serious about improving themselves.

About Coach Austin

My name is Coach Austin, and I am excited for the opportunity to get to know you and help you achieve your goals. I am a NASM Certified Personal Trainer and hold a bachelor's degree in Exercise Science and Kinesiology with a strong passion for fitness, performance, and long-term development.

I played five years of college soccer in Tampa, Florida, before moving to Poland to continue my career playing professional soccer. Through my experience in sport and training, I developed a deep appreciation for fitness — not only as a way to improve physically, but as a way to build discipline, confidence, and resilience.

I believe that character is bigger than anything we achieve physically. Fitness is the tool, but growth in character, consistency, and mindset is what creates lasting success both in and out of the gym.
